Glitch Collage

Tool(s): Photoshop, Illustrator

Fresh donut with pink icing and sprinkles are my favorite. For this collage, I used an image of a donut that I had previously glitched using Photoshop. I then took the image to Illustrator, where I divided the image equally into squares and rearranged them in a random order while keeping the shape of a large square.
