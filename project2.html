Movie Poster Design

Tool(s): Illustrator

Learning how to play chess is not for the weak...neither is designing a movie poster based on it. I searched many images related to chess. I found the back ground image and decided to make a black and white image trace from a color palette. I then image traced the hand and chess pieces as well. I took the hand from another image and placed a queen piece between the fingers to look as if it's holding the piece. All colors in this poster are from one color palette.
