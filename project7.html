Animated Landscape

Tool(s): Premire Pro, Adobe Stock, Illustrator

Often you can't see the bottom of the ocean. We also don't know what is down there. Well, Mr. Goose here has a submarine and is exploring the depths to show us what he found. I found several images on Adobe Stock, isolated them on Illustrator, and collaged them onto Premire Pro. I added several moving components into the video to make all the sea life swim around.
